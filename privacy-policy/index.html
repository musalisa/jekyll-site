Thereminvox takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

Information Collection

Thereminvox respects and protects the privacy of the individuals that use its services. Thereminvox does not collect any unique information about you (such as your name, email address, etc.) unless you register with Thereminvox. The data required for registration (name, surname, email address) are used only for Thereminvox services ("Library", "Forum") to function and are not disclosed to any third party. They will never be used for any other purposes without your consent.

Thereminvox automatically collects information about your usage of the site (including time of day, browser type, browser type and language, IP address, pages you request and cookie information). Yet that information is used only for statistical reasons and in an aggregated way, not connected to single users.

Some services within Thereminvox.com such as the Forums and the Library, display the members name and message but not the email address. It's up to you to disclose any personal information such as email address.

Links to other sites and Advertising

Thereminvox contains links and advertising banners to other sites. Thereminvox is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. Advertising Banners and links are supplied by a number of companies, including the following: Amazon.com, Amazon.co.uk, zZounds.com, Sheetmusicplus.com. (please, read their privacy policy). In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertisers may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser.

Children's Online Privacy

No information should be submitted to or posted at Thereminvox by visitors under the age 13 without the consent of their parents or guardians.

Editing and Deleting Your Account Information

You can always edit your account information by clicking on the "Edit user" button in the left vertical menu. If you desire your account to be permanently deleted from our database write to info@thereminvox.com .

Confidentiality and Security

We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

Thereminvox may update its privacy policy. We will notify you about changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the email address specified in your Thereminvox account. This statement and the policies outlined here are not intended to and do not give you any contractual or other legal rights.

Contacting Thereminvox

If you have any questions about this privacy statement or any other subject related to this website you can contact us at info@thereminvox.com .
