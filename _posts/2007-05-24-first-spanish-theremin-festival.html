The First Spanish Theremin Festival will be held on Saturday June 2, 2007 in Barcelona and Friday, June 8, 2007 in Salamanca. Lydia Kavina will head the second part of the festival.

[caption id="attachment_488" align="alignright" width="300"] Logo of the "Primer Festival Hispano de Theremin."[/caption]

The First Spanish Theremin Festival will be held on Saturday June, 2 2007 in Barcelona and Friday, June 8, 2007 in Salamanca.

The first day (Barcelona, June 2, 2007 ), organized by Victor Estrada, will be devoted to the projection of theremin movies, theremin demonstrations, and will conclude with theremin recitals by spanish thereminists Victor Estrada, Jairo Moreno, Quico Palomar and the "Pentatonika Barat" duo.

The second day (Salamanca, June 8, 2007), directed by Lydia Kavina, will be dedicated to a theremin workshop conducted by Lydia Kavina and, in the evening, the electronic performance "Theremin" by Hotel Pro Forma, featuring Lydia Kavina.
