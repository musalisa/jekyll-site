Five fabolous videos of Jean Jacques Perrey playing live with Dana Countryman, being interviewed and lecturing with Bob Moog.

If you, like me, are not among the lucky ones who attended Jean Jacques Perrey and Dana Countryman gigs in California, you can comfort yourself by looking at these fabolous videos by Mal Meehan at the Jean Jacques Perrey's mini-site (QuickTime 7 required).

[caption id="attachment_666" align="aligncenter" width="600"] Jean Jacques Perrey playing the Ondioline at the Club Transmediale Festival 2006 (Germany).[/caption]

For your listening and viewing pleasure there are three classic Perrey songs (Corn on the Cob, E.V.A. and La funambule De L'Espace) played live by Jean Jacques and Dana at the Club Transmediale Festival 2006 (Berlin).

Then there is a beautiful and informative interview conducted by Richard Lawson.

And, finally, an amusing lecture given in 2004 by Jean Jacques along with Bob Moog at the Tryptych Festival (Scotland).

Very good stuff!

And, by the way, if you live in Europe you (we) have a second chance, since Jean Jacques will play live at the Todaysart Festival 2006 (Den Haag) on September 22, 2006.
