Clara Rockmore's 1936 Rockport, Maine concert program posted by Reid Welch to the Levnet mailing list in 1997.

C O N C E R T

Rockport Town Hall
FRIDAY EVENING, SEPTEMBER 11, 1936
Benefit Children's Christmas Welfare Fund
The Artists:
Shura Cherkassky, Pianist
Clara Rockmore, Thereminist
Nadia Reisenberg, at the piano
Edward H. Rhein, Baritone
Edith Evans Braun, at the piano
(overleaf)

P R O G R A M

 

Prelude and Fugue F Minor J.S. Bach
Nocturne E flat major, Op. 55, No. 2 Frederic Chopin
Kaliedascop Josef Hofmann
Shura Cherkassky

 

Zueignung Richard Strauss
Er Ist's Hugo Wolf
Lob des Liedens Richard Strauss
Edward H. Rhein

 

Aria Johan Mattheson (1681-1764)
Melodie Gluck-Kreisler
Andante (from Symphonie Espagnol) Edouard Lalo
Clara Rockmore
I N T E R M I S S I O N
(facing page)

 

Old Mother Hubbard Victor Hely-Hutchinson (set in the manner of Handel)
When I have sung my songs Ernest Charles
The Bitterness of Love James Dunn
Captain Stratton's Fancy Deems Taylor
Edward H. Rhein

 

Chant du Menestrel Alexander Glazounoff
Berceuse Peter I. Tschaikowsky
Romaine Henri Wieniawski
Clara Rockmore

 

"Wine, Women and Song" Strauss-Godowsky (Waltz paraphrase)
Shura Cherkassky
,.,.,.,.,.,
The piano is a Steinway

Note from Reid: I concert-tuned and voiced Cherkassky's piano for his last recital here in Miami in 1992. 'Twas still a Steinway.
