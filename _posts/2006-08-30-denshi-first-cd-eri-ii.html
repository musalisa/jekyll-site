Japanese thereminist Eri Ii has released "Denshi On", her first CD.

A nice mixture of easy listening, japanese music and electronica, Denshi On has been all played on Moog Etherwave Pro and Moog Synthesizer.

The final track "Shin Nihon Kikou" has been composed by Isao Tomita.

"Denshi On" is available at CD Baby.
