Asheville, NC Come visit the beautiful mountains and join us Thursday, August 4 though Sunday, August 7, 2005 for a one-of-a-kind event.

Thereminists and theremin enthusiasts from around the world are invited for 3 days of interactive workshops and stellar performances that will excite, entertain, and inform.

Featuring performances by Lydia Kavina, Pamelia Kurstin, Barbara Bucholz, Armen Ra and an open mic night!

A complete schedule and registration details can be found at www.ethermusic2005.com

Enter the Original Composition Contest

If you have an original theremin composition, we have an opportunity for you! The Orange Peel is sponsoring an Original Theremin Composition Contest in conjunction with Ether Music 2005. Enter your composition and you might be the winner of the $1000 Grand Prize or receive one of five free registrations to Ether Music 2005. Amateur and professional theremin composers click the link above for details. Submit soon as entries must be postmarked no later than June 17, 2005.

Presented by The Orange Peel and sponsored by Moog Music.
